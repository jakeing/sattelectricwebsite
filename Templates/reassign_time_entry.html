About Reassignment

Reassigning a time entry will move it from its current work order to the selected target work order. This operation cannot be undone.

All time entry details (engineer, date, time, hours) will remain unchanged - only the associated work order will be updated.

Important: Ensure that you select the correct work order for reassignment. This will affect reporting and billing.
