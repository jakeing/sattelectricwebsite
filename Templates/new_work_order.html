Quick Tips

  • Fields marked with * are required.

  • Use Estimated Hours to track time budgets.
  • Higher Priority work orders will appear first in dashboards.
Status Explanation

Open: Work that has not been completed

Complete: Work that is finished but not yet closed

Closed: Work that is finished and administratively complete
